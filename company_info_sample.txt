The company supports clothing brands, apparel businesses, and textile customers by providing fabric-related solutions for product development and production. This sample knowledge base is created for a prototype AI customer inquiry assistant. Company Background:
The company is based in Foshan, China, an important manufacturing region in the Pearl River Delta. The region is close to Hong Kong and Macao and has convenient access to transportation and international business channels. The company has experience serving customers in the textile and apparel industry and focuses on practical support for fabric development, production communication, and customer service.

The company mainly focuses on denim fabric and related textile materials. It supports customers who need fabrics for jeans, casualwear, fashion apparel, and other clothing products. The company’s business is not only about selling fabric, but also about helping customers understand material options, development needs, production support, and possible textile solutions.

Lighter fabrics may be more suitable for shirts or summer clothing, while heavier fabrics may be more suitable for jeans, jackets, or structured garments. The assistant should ask the customer about the target product, season, comfort needs, and expected fabric hand feel.
